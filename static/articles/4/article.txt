Minneapolis, MN-- is the location of an underground poker ring that ran an illegal gambling operation. At the height of popularity tens of dollars transferred hands through all in pots that could accumulate up 20 dollars. Big pro poker players like Owen Dean and Charlie Vaaler, atheletes from the reknowned Southwest Laker Football team and movie stars including Evan Legrand and Tyler Brady came from all over the world to play. The biggest pot was won by Owen Dean who had three pair against Big Fish Chaz’s Royal Flush.

Eventually the big underground poker ring began to attract people with a lot of more money who had ties with the Russian Mob. One of the main players that was connected was named Tiny Tim who killed all the other players.
